- [QT25] (Moderate)  (#2)

- [KN3] (Low)  (#3 #27)
   • Dẫn chứng: Chat #3: Andy says 'I have set up the working hour from 9-5 for you' and 'we shall set up the status and the auto-reply message' then moves directly to email setup without asking customer to verify working hours. Chat #27: similar pattern — applies fix, screenshots, moves on without 'Could you try it on your end?'

- [KN7] (Low)  (#23)
