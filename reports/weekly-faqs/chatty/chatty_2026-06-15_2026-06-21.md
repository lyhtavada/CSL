## 1. AI Agent — Setup & Training

### Q1: How do I set up and train the AI agent for the first time? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~40 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Advanced AI features (Store Instructions, Scenarios, Q&A, expanded Data Sources) require a **paid plan**. Three steps: (1) **AI agent → Training data → Manage** to add Products, Collections, Discounts, Markets, FAQs, and Custom knowledge; (2) **AI agent → Instructions → Manage** to set the AI's role, tone, and behaviours; (3) **AI agent → Test AI → Test now** to check responses before going live (the Test feature does **not** consume your AI-replies quota). Many "the AI is just guessing" complaints are stores where Training data and Instructions were never filled in — start there.

### Q2: How many products can the AI train on / "it won't upload all my products"? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~12 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Product sync limits by plan: **Free 100, Basic 500, Pro 8,000, Plus 20,000**. If products are missing in **AI agent → Training data → Manage → Products**, first check that they aren't **deactivated** in the list (toggle them Active) and that no search/filter is hiding them. If the header says "X of X products learned" but the list shows "No items found," clear any active filter and switch to the **All** tab — if it's still empty, escalate as a display bug. Stores with more than 5,000 products need manual backend setup — contact support.

### Q3: Can your team set the AI up for me? (Done-for-you setup) 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Yes. On **Pro and Plus plans**, our team can do a guided/done-for-you setup of your AI agent at no extra cost — we enable the right data sources (Products, Collections, Discounts, store policy pages), review your Instructions, and configure human handover. We'll set up alongside you and always recommend testing in **AI agent → Test AI** before turning the AI on live, especially for high-traffic stores. Tell us your plan and we'll get started.

### Q4: How do I create a custom scenario / make the AI follow a specific flow? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Go to **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → Assistant skills → Custom scenarios → + Add scenario**, then set a scenario name (the topic customers mention) and the instructions (the flow you want). Use it for returns, order lookups, "ask for email before tracking," sizing, or any process you want handled consistently. Scenario count is limited by plan (Free/Basic 5, Pro 15, Plus unlimited); if you hit the limit, contact support with your use cases.

### Q5: Can I upload files (PDF/CSV) or add my website to train the AI? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~5 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Yes — **AI agent → Training data → Manage → Custom knowledge → Add data → Files** (PDF, CSV, TXT, JSON, max 2MB each; CSV is best for structured Q&A). For website content, add individual page **URLs** (the domain alone only trains on the homepage). Shopify policy pages (shipping, returns, privacy) can be synced as URLs too. The AI does **not** auto-scrape your whole site — you choose what to feed it.

### Q6: I ran out of AI conversations — can I add more? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~5 sessions
**Standard answer:**
AI conversation limits per plan: **Free 50 lifetime, Basic 50/mo, Pro 300/mo, Plus 700/mo**; paid plans charge **$0.40 per extra conversation** beyond the monthly limit. Check usage at **Chatty → Subscription → View details** (resets on your billing cycle date, not the calendar month). To control spend, set a cap via **View details → Change limit**. When the Free limit is hit the AI deactivates, but human agents can still reply in the Inbox. CS can top up a small bonus in special cases — confirm plan + usage first.

## 3. AI — Wrong / Hallucinated Responses

### Q8: The AI is giving wrong info / making things up (e.g. promising return labels, recommending products we don't sell). 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
First, the fastest fix is to add the correct fact to your training: put the exact policy in **AI agent → Training data → Manage → Custom knowledge** (Q&A) and/or add a behaviour/scenario under **Instructions → Manage** (e.g. "We do not provide return labels; customers cover return shipping; offer partial refunds case-by-case"). For wrong-product recommendations or info that persists after correcting the data, send the **conversation ID** to support — the team adds an instruction hook to correct the response logic. Removing a phone number the AI keeps offering: delete it from Custom knowledge **and** add an instruction not to share it.

### Q9: The AI's name / welcome message shows the wrong text or wrong language (still says "Chatty" after I renamed it). 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~5 sessions
**Standard answer:**
The bot name and welcome message live in **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → General instructions** (Role + welcome message). If you changed it but the storefront still shows the old name or English on a non-English store, the **translation version is "Outdated"**: go to **Settings → Translation**, find the language, click **Auto-translate**, Save, and confirm the status flips from "Outdated" to "Updated." Make sure the name is consistent in both the Role/Instructions and the welcome message. For Behaviours, add "Always respond in the same language as the customer."

## 4. Human Handover & AI Availability

### Q10: How do I control when the AI hands a conversation to a human? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Go to **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → Assistant skills → Customer support skills → Human handover**. Under **"When should AI hand off to your team?"**: "Customer asks to talk to a person" is always on; "AI cannot answer," "Customer repeats question," and "Negative sentiment" are toggles — enable the ones you want. Add **Intent rules** for topics that should transfer immediately (e.g. cancellations). Under **"Where does the customer go?"** pick **Transfer to human in Chatty inbox** (agent joins the same chat), **Collect info & follow up by email**, or **Show contact methods**.

### Q11: I want the AI to only reply when my agents are offline (so I can answer myself). 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Set the AI to defer to your team: **AI agent → Settings → AI availability** and choose **"Only when agents are offline."** With that on, you handle chats while online and the AI covers off-hours. You can always jump into any conversation manually from the Inbox even while the AI is active. If you instead want the AI to keep replying after handover only when the team is offline, that's the **"Allow only outside business hours"** option under Human handover → offline behavior.

### Q12: My team shows "offline" even though someone is logged in / how do agents log in and get alerted to a chat? 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~5 sessions
**Standard answer:**
"Offline" while logged in is usually **Away mode** on that member — turn it off so they show online to customers. Agents log in at **app.chatty.net** (no Shopify admin needed; they accept the invite email first and set a password). For alerts: enable in-app notifications and, importantly, **allow browser notifications from Chatty** in the agent's browser; you can also set email alerts for high-priority cases. Online/offline status only works when the **Live chat** block is on in Chatbox settings. Working hours are set in **Settings → General → Chat availability → Manage**.

### Q13: The AI told the customer a human would join but no one did / handover routing. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~4 sessions
**Standard answer:**
If you have no live agents, don't use "Transfer to inbox" as the destination — switch to **Collect info & follow up by email** under Human handover → "Where does the customer go?" so the AI clearly tells the customer they'll get an email reply (set a **Response time promise**). If you do use Transfer to inbox, configure the offline behavior ("Leave a message" + response-time promise) so customers aren't left waiting on a phantom live agent. Confirm the support email is set so handover summaries reach your team.

## 5. Widget / Chatbox

### Q14: The chat widget isn't showing / disappeared on my store. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Sequential check: (1) if it worked before, toggle the **App Embed** off/on — **Shopify Admin → Online Store → Themes → Customize → App Embeds → Chatty** → OFF → Save → ON → Save, then reload the storefront; (2) confirm **App Embed ON** and **Chatbox → General → Blocks → Live chat** toggled on. If it's only meant to show on some pages, check **Chatbox → Advanced → Display settings → Display pages**. If still missing, use the collaborator-access flow and escalate to TS with store URL, theme name, and steps tried.

### Q15: The chat button/navbar looks wrong on mobile or overlaps another element. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~7 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Collect first: which element/page, device, and a screenshot or recording. Self-serve fix for position — **Chatbox → Appearance → Chatbox button → Position**: drag the dot or set an **Anchor** + **Horizontal/Vertical offset** (0–45%). For mobile-specific layout/whitespace bugs inside the chatbox navbar that the Position control can't fix, escalate to TS with the recording — these are handled with a targeted CSS fix (we did this successfully this week).

### Q16: How do I limit Chatty to specific pages? 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~3 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Go to **Chatbox → Advanced → Display settings → Display pages** and choose "Show on specific pages" (or hide on specific pages), then select the pages. Save and reload the storefront to confirm.

## 6. AI Data Coverage

### Q17: My custom (non-standard) product metafields aren't synced to the AI. 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~2 sessions
**Standard answer:**
You can see and toggle synced metafields at **AI agent → Training data → Products → Manage metafields**. However, **non-standard / third-party custom metafields** (e.g. created via a metafields app) are **not currently supported** — only the supported metafield types sync. See the supported list in the help docs (Data sources → product metafields). As a workaround, add that data into **Custom knowledge** as Q&A. If it's important to your store, we can log a **feature request** to the product team — share your use case.

## 7. FAQ Page

### Q18: My FAQ page / categories aren't showing (only some appear). 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~5 sessions
**Standard answer:**
(1) Enable it: **FAQs → FAQs page → Display FAQs page → ON** and Save; (2) on the same page, turn on **"Embed app to your theme"** if Off; (3) add the FAQ page URL (default `/pages/avada-faqs`) to your nav via **Shopify Admin → Content → Menus**. If the page looks empty in the Shopify page editor, that's normal — the content is rendered by the Chatty app, not stored in the Shopify page. Manage actual questions at **Chatty → FAQs → Manage FAQs**.

### Q19: How do I set up FAQs (page vs. embedded block vs. AI Q&A)? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~20 sessions
**Standard answer:**
There are a few options, depending on your goal: (1) a dedicated **FAQs page** on your store (`/pages/avada-faqs`); (2) a **FAQs block** embedded on a specific page/section; or (3) FAQ content used to **train the AI** so it answers in chat. Add/edit questions and categories under **Chatty → FAQs → Manage FAQs**. Tell us which one you want and we'll point you to the exact toggle.

## 8. Email Channel & Notifications

### Q20: My email channel is stuck "Unverified" / AI isn't replying to emails. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Email connects by **forwarding**: in **Settings → Channels → (your email) → Manage**, copy the Chatty forwarding address (e.g. `…@chattyemail.com`) and add a **forwarding rule in your own email provider** (Gmail, GoDaddy, etc.) pointing your support inbox to that address — verification only completes once forwarding is set up on the provider side, not just in Chatty. Outlook/organization mail may block auto-forwarding (IT admin must allow it). To enable AI on email, toggle the Email channel under **AI agent → Settings → AI channels**; the AI auto-replies after ~5 minutes if no agent responds. If forwarding is correctly set and it still won't verify, send a screenshot of the forwarding rule and escalate to dev.

### Q21: Order tracking shows the wrong/Chinese carrier, or I want my own tracking page instead of 17TRACK. 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~3 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Choose the tracking method that fits: **Chatbox → General → turn on Order tracking**, then **Settings → Integrations → Order tracking → Manage** and pick **Default tracking** (major carriers), **Custom tracking** (redirect to your own tracking URL, e.g. a Track123 page), or **17TRACK** (on-site embed). If you want customers redirected to your own page and not see the raw carrier, use **Custom tracking** with your tracking URL. Note: hiding a specific carrier name in the AI's tracking reply, or forcing a third-party page in place of the built-in flow, needs a custom scenario / backend tweak — collect the desired URL and an example order and escalate to TS.

## 9. Pricing, Plans & Refunds

### Q22: How much is Chatty / what's on each plan? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~12 sessions
**Standard answer:**
- **Free** $0 — 50 AI conversations (lifetime), 100 products, 1 team member, 1 email channel, 90-day history.
- **Basic** $19.99/mo (annual $16.99) — 50 AI conv/mo + $0.40/extra, 500 products, 5 team members, unlimited email channels, 12-month history, full chatbox customization.
- **Pro** $68.99/mo (annual $58.99) — 300 AI conv/mo + $0.40/extra, 8,000 products, 10 team members, full AI channels + analytics, unlimited history.
- **Plus** $199/mo (annual $169.99) — 700 AI conv/mo + $0.40/extra, 20,000 products, unlimited team members, advanced analytics, dedicated AI consultant.
- **Enterprise** — custom.
All paid plans include a **7-day free trial**. Confirm the merchant's current plan in Shopify, then map their need (product count, AI volume, team seats, languages) to the right tier.

### Q23: How do I cancel / downgrade, and can I get a refund? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~7 sessions
**Standard answer:**
To stop charges, downgrade to Free: **Chatty → Subscription → View plans → Free plan** (do it before your next billing cycle; your data/settings are kept). A **30-day money-back guarantee** applies if you didn't continue after the first month, cancelled before the first cycle ended, or accidentally chose a paid plan without using it. For a refund, collect a **screenshot of the Shopify charge** (app name, billing cycle, amount). Per CS process, **CS cannot approve refunds without CS Leader sign-off** — resolve the underlying issue first where possible, then create a Trello card for the CS Leader. Refunds take 7–10 business days once approved.

## 10. WhatsApp / Messenger / Instagram Channels

### Q24: My WhatsApp number is stuck on "Pending" / won't connect. 🔁 recurring (frequency UP)
**Frequency:** ~20 sessions
**Standard answer:**
"Pending" means Meta verification is still in progress — usually **24–48 hours**. Requirements: a **Meta Business Manager** with **Business Admin** role (not Employee), a valid WhatsApp number not active on the personal WhatsApp app or another API provider (Twilio/360dialog must be released first), completed **Meta Business Verification**, **2FA enabled** on the number, and all permissions granted to "AVADA group company limited" during OAuth (no popup/cookie blockers). If it's still Pending after 48 hours with all of the above confirmed, escalate with: phone number last 4 digits, Meta Business Manager ID, when connected, and a screenshot of the pending status. Note: the Pending state itself is managed on Meta's side, not within Chatty.

### Q25: Messenger/Instagram messages aren't showing in my inbox, or the AI isn't replying to them. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Connect under **Settings → Channels** (Instagram must be a business account linked to a Facebook Page; the connecting user needs "Manage and access Page messages"). Only messages received **after** connecting will sync — old messages stay in Meta. To get **AI responses** on Messenger/Instagram, that's a separate step: **AI agent → Settings → AI channels** and toggle the channel on. Replies you make directly inside Facebook/Instagram (not via Chatty) may not sync back — a known limitation. If all permissions are correct and messages still don't appear, escalate with store URL, page name/ID, and a Channels screenshot.

## 11. New Install / Upgrade Confusion

### Q26: After upgrading, my FAQ page looks blank / my FAQ content was "wiped." 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~3 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Upgrading does **not** delete your FAQ content. Two things cause this impression: (1) the Shopify page editor shows the FAQ page as blank because the content is rendered by the Chatty app, not stored in the Shopify page — check the live storefront URL instead; and (2) on a **new install**, Chatty seeds a small **sample/default FAQ** as a starting point, which can be mistaken for your "real" FAQs being replaced. Your own questions live in **Chatty → FAQs → Manage FAQs** — edit/add there. The Chatty FAQ page is optional; if you already have your own FAQ page, you can keep it and instead feed its URL into **AI agent → Training data → Custom knowledge**. (We should never ask for a merchant's store password — collaborator access only, when investigation is genuinely needed.)
