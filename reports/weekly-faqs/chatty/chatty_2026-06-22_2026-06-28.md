## 1. AI Agent — Setup & Training (incl. Done-for-You)

### Q1: How do I set up and train the AI agent for the first time? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~60 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Advanced AI features (Store Instructions, Scenarios, Q&A, expanded Data Sources) require a **paid plan**. Three steps: (1) **AI agent → Training data → Manage** to add Products, Collections, Discounts, Markets, FAQs, and Custom knowledge; (2) **AI agent → Instructions → Manage** to set the AI's role, tone, welcome message, and behaviours; (3) **AI agent → Test AI → Test now** to check responses before going live (the Test feature does **not** consume your AI-replies quota). Most "the AI is generic / just says hi" complaints are stores where Training data and Instructions were never filled in — start there, then **Activate** the AI agent so it works on the live storefront (not only in Test).

### Q2: Can your team set the AI up for me? (Done-for-you setup) 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~50 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Yes — this is by far the most common request. Our team can do a guided/done-for-you setup at no extra cost: we enable the right data sources (Products, Collections, Discounts, store policy pages), set the AI's **name, welcome message, tone**, configure **human handover** (transfer vs. email follow-up), and add Custom knowledge / FAQs. To do it we'll typically ask for: the assistant **name + greeting**, your **support email**, **availability** (24/7 or only when you're offline), **tone**, which topics should go to a human (refunds, complaints, pricing), and your **policy/FAQ page URLs**. We always recommend testing in **AI agent → Test AI** and then **Activating** the agent. Note: most setup is done with the merchant in-chat — we never need the store password (collaborator access only, when investigation is genuinely required).

### Q3: How many products can the AI train on / "it won't sync my products" / "no items found." 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~12 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Product sync limits by plan: **Free 100, Basic 500, Pro 8,000, Plus 20,000**. On a fresh install sync can take a few minutes — give it time and refresh. If products are missing in **AI agent → Training data → Manage → Products**, check they aren't set **Inactive** (the inactive tab holds everything beyond the plan limit or anything toggled off) and clear any search/filter. You can multi-select to bulk activate/deactivate. If the header says "X products learned" but the list shows "No items found," clear the filter and switch to the **All** tab; if still empty, escalate as a display bug. Stores over ~5,000 products may need manual backend setup — contact support.

### Q4: How do I create a custom scenario / make the AI follow a specific flow (e.g. order cancellation, custom orders)? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Go to **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → Assistant skills → Custom scenarios → + Add scenario**, set a scenario name (the topic customers mention) and the step-by-step instructions. Use it for order cancellation, returns, "ask for email/order number before tracking," custom-order intake, sizing, or any process you want handled consistently. Scenario count is limited by plan (Free/Basic 5, Pro 15, Plus unlimited); some merchants reported scenarios "disappearing" after a downgrade — counts above your plan limit are deactivated, so confirm the plan. If a scenario isn't followed reliably (multi-step flows can be inconsistent), keep it concise, test in **Test AI**, and send the conversation ID if it still won't follow the steps.

### Q5: Can I upload files (PDF/CSV) or add my website/FAQ page to train the AI? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Yes — **AI agent → Training data → Manage → Custom knowledge → Add data → Files** (PDF, CSV, TXT, JSON, max 2MB each; CSV is best for structured Q&A). For website content, add individual page **URLs** (the domain alone only trains on the homepage); we can pull your existing FAQ page this way (e.g. `/pages/avada-faqs`). If a CSV import shows questions as **"Failed,"** it's usually because the topic was set to **Published but not Active** — set the topic active, fix the file, and re-upload; if the app won't let you delete the failed rows, contact support to clear them. The AI does not auto-scrape your whole site — you choose what to feed it.

### Q6: I ran out of AI conversations — what counts as a conversation, and can I add more? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
AI conversation limits per plan: **Free 50 (lifetime, not monthly), Basic 50/mo, Pro 300/mo, Plus 700/mo**; paid plans charge **$0.40 per extra conversation** beyond the monthly limit (set/raise this under your overage limit). A "conversation" is a chat session, not a single reply. Check usage at **Chatty → Subscription → View details** (resets on your billing-cycle date). When the limit is hit the AI deactivates (it may toggle off again if it keeps hitting the cap), but human agents can still reply in the Inbox. Unused conversations do **not** carry over to next month. CS can top up a small bonus in special cases — confirm plan + usage first. Note: several long-time Free users this week were surprised the Free tier is **50 lifetime** (it does not reset monthly) — that's current behaviour.

## 2. 404 / Inbox Crash & Cross-Contamination Defect ⚠️

### Q7: I get "404 / page not found / technical problem with Avada app" when I open a chat or the inbox; old resolved chats reappear; new chats land in Resolved; two customers show in one thread. ⚠️ 🆕/🔁 (frequency sharply UP)
**Frequency:** ~25+ sessions
**Standard answer:**
This is a recognized **system-side defect**, not a merchant setting. First-line: have them **hard-refresh / reload** (it often loads after a few tries) and try an incognito window — that clears the transient 404 for some. If it persists, or you see any of: **old/resolved conversations reappearing as unread**, **new chats landing in the Resolved tab**, the **Resolve button showing "Reopen" and not closing**, the inbox **crashing mid-chat**, or **two different customers' messages in one thread** — collect **conversation/session IDs, affected customer emails, a screenshot or Loom, the store URL, and timestamps**, then escalate to the dev team. Tell the merchant: "Thanks for flagging — this is a system-side issue our team is investigating; I've logged your details so they can trace it." Do **not** advise reinstalling. **Internal note for Liz:** this is the top escalation of the week by volume and was the explicit canned opener ("Please help me fix 404 page") in ~25+ sessions; the cross-contamination + mis-routing variants confirm it's not fixed. Keep on the dev radar as P1.

## 3. AI — "No access to my data" / Wrong or Hallucinated Responses

### Q8: The AI gives wrong info / makes things up (wrong payment provider, expired discount, product we don't sell, dead links). 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Fastest fix: add the correct fact to your training and override the wrong one. Put the exact policy/answer in **AI agent → Training data → Manage → Custom knowledge** (Q&A) and add a behaviour/scenario under **Instructions → Manage** stating the correct fact (e.g. "We offer Klarna, not Scalapay"; "Do not mention any discount codes unless currently active"). For wrong info that **persists after you've corrected the data** (e.g. a stale promo the AI keeps quoting, a 404 product link, recommending another store's products), send the **conversation ID** to support — the team traces the source and adds an instruction hook. Tip from this week: write the **correct** statement rather than "don't say X," since negative phrasing can leak into replies.

### Q9: The AI replies "I don't have access to that information" even though it's in my product pages / FAQ metafields / size chart / Custom knowledge. 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~15 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Work through these in order: (1) confirm the product is **Active** in **AI agent → Training data → Products** and that sync finished (it can lag a few minutes after install/upload); (2) standard product fields (title, description, variants, price, availability) and **supported metafields** sync — toggle them in **Products → Manage metafields**. However, **product-page "FAQ" / spec metafields and many third-party/custom metafields are not reliably indexed** today, so technical specs stored only in those tabs may not reach the AI; (3) **images don't get read** — a size chart saved as an image won't be parsed, so re-enter that data as text in **Custom knowledge** (Q&A) or a CSV; (4) for FAQ/Q&A content, make sure the FAQ/Q&A topic is **active** and the question is phrased plainly. As a catch-all, put the missing facts into **Custom knowledge** and re-test in **Test AI**. If correctly-synced data still isn't used, send the conversation ID — and we can log a **feature request** for FAQ/custom-metafield sync (raised again this week for a 450-product technical store).

## 4. Human Handover & AI Availability

### Q10: I want to answer customers myself — only let the AI reply when I'm offline (or turn the AI off entirely). 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~15 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Two setups depending on your goal. To **answer everything yourself with the AI as backup**: set **AI agent → Settings → AI availability → "Only when agents are offline."** You stay online and handle chats; the AI covers off-hours, and you can jump into any chat from the Inbox while the AI is active. To use **live chat only with no AI at all**: deactivate the AI agent and keep the **Live chat** block on in Chatbox settings — customers then talk to you directly. If you only want the AI to defer to humans after a handover when the team is offline, that's the offline option under Human handover. Many merchants this week just wanted "collect their email and tell them I'll reply when back online" — that's the **Collect info & follow up by email** handover destination (see Q13).

### Q11: The AI keeps replying after my agent has joined / taken over the conversation (it talks over us). 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
When an agent clicks **Join** on a conversation, the AI is meant to step back and let the human take over. This week several merchants reported the AI continuing to reply after a human joined, especially right after a transfer or when the chat wasn't formally **assigned** to the agent. Workaround: make sure the agent **Joins/assigns** the conversation (not just types), and confirm AI availability isn't forcing the AI to always reply. If the AI still posts over a joined agent, this is a recognized issue — collect the **conversation ID, the agent who joined, and whether the chat was assigned**, and escalate to the dev team. Tell the merchant we've logged it as a defect.

### Q12: The AI's product links use the `.myshopify.com` URL instead of my real domain. 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~5 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Collection links and page links generally use your **primary domain**, but several merchants this week found **product links specifically** falling back to the `vendor.myshopify.com` URL (e.g. `vckad6-mh.myshopify.com/products/...` instead of `yourstore.com/...`). Confirm your **primary domain is set in Shopify → Settings → Domains**, then re-sync products. If product links still show the myshopify URL after that, it's a known issue on our side — send the conversation ID and an example product link and we'll escalate; the team can correct the product-URL handling. (Logged as product feedback this week.)

### Q13: The AI told the customer a human would join but no one did / I have no live agents. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
If you don't keep live agents online, don't use "Transfer to inbox" as the handover destination — switch to **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → Assistant skills → Customer support skills → Human handover → "Where does the customer go?" → Collect info & follow up by email**, and set a **Response time promise**. That way the AI clearly tells the customer they'll get an email reply instead of leaving them waiting on a phantom live agent. If you *do* sometimes staff live chat, set the **offline behavior** so it routes to email when no agent is online. Confirm the support email is set so handover summaries actually reach your team. A couple of merchants reported the setting "not sticking" (AI still said "our team will follow up within 24h") — if changing the destination doesn't take effect after save + refresh, escalate with the conversation ID.

## 5. Widget / Chatbox

### Q14: I want the chat to open straight into the AI chat (skip the home/pre-chat screen) so customers can type right away. 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
A few things control this. The "home" screen and conversation starters live in **Chatbox** settings — you can trim or remove conversation starters so the box opens cleaner. If customers are being asked for **name/email before they can type**, that's the **pre-chat form**: turn it off (or allow "chat as anonymous") so they reach the AI immediately. Make sure the **Live chat / AI block** is enabled so the message box appears (several merchants saw "no option to type" because the relevant block was off or the display style was mid-change). If you want a specific minimal layout beyond the built-in controls, share a screenshot and TS can advise on Custom CSS.

### Q15: The chat button is in the wrong spot / overlaps Add-to-Cart, especially on mobile. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~9 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Self-serve fix for position — **Chatbox → Appearance → Chatbox button → Position**: drag the dot or set an **Anchor** + **Horizontal/Vertical offset** (0–45%). Important caveat from this week: the **Position control sometimes only moves the desktop button, not mobile** — several merchants couldn't shift the mobile icon or hide it on mobile via the standard settings. For mobile-only overlap (e.g. covering the add-to-cart button or a back-to-top button on product pages), collect a screenshot/recording, device, and page, and escalate to TS — these are resolved with a targeted CSS fix (done successfully multiple times this week).

### Q16: How do I change the widget colour / button icon / response-bubble colour / AI name? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Widget colour, button shape/icon and bubble styling are under **Chatbox → Appearance**; the AI's **name, welcome message and avatar** are under **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → General instructions**. Common pain this week: the colour picker has **no preview of the message-bubble text contrast**, so merchants picked dark colours where text became unreadable — pick a light bubble or have us set it. To change the **AI name** so the storefront stops saying "Chatty," update both the Role/Instructions and the welcome message. Custom widget colour is available on paid plans (a Basic trial unlocks it). Logged as product feedback: add a live preview of bubble/text colour, and a resize control for logos in email.

## 6. FAQ Page & Blocks

### Q17: My FAQ page / FAQ block is blank or not showing on the store. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
For the **FAQ page**: (1) **FAQs → FAQs page → Display FAQs page → ON** and Save; (2) turn on **"Embed app to your theme"**; (3) add the page URL (default `/pages/avada-faqs`) to your nav via **Shopify Admin → Content → Menus**. The page looks blank in the Shopify page editor because the content is rendered by the Chatty app, not stored in the Shopify page — check the live storefront URL. For the **FAQ block** on a specific page: enable **Display FAQs block**, set the display condition/pages, and crucially **enable the Chatty app block in that theme** (a recurring gotcha is the block working on the live theme but not on a draft theme until the app is enabled there too). Actual questions/categories are managed at **Chatty → FAQs → Manage FAQs**; "featured/starred" questions are what surface first and in the chatbot.

### Q18: My FAQ formatting (line spacing/bullets) looks broken on the storefront. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~3 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Line breaks and spacing added in the FAQ editor don't always render on the storefront, and bullets can sit off to the left — a known rich-text limitation. Use plain text rather than pasted HTML. For consistent spacing/indentation across all FAQs, this is best fixed with a small CSS adjustment from our side: share your FAQ page URL and we'll apply it (done this week). We've logged the inconsistent rich-text rendering as product feedback. If you'd rather keep your own FAQ page, you can disable Chatty's FAQ page and instead feed your FAQ page URL into **AI agent → Training data → Custom knowledge**.

## 7. Inbox, Conversations & CRM Use

### Q19: How do I export or bulk-download my conversation history? 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
In the app you can export an **individual conversation**: open it → **⋮ menu (top right) → Export transcript**. There's **no bulk date-range export in the UI** today — for multiple conversations or a date range (several merchants asked for, e.g., "May 1–Jun 23" or "Jun 15–20"), contact support with the **date range and the email to send it to**, and the team will export and send the data (CSV). If you run on Zendesk, resolved Chatty conversations are saved as Zendesk tickets automatically. (Zoho Desk integration was requested this week and is not currently available — logged as feedback.)

### Q20: Can the AI draft/suggest an email reply for me to review before it's sent? 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~3 sessions
**Standard answer:**
A **"Suggest reply"** capability is in **Beta** — it drafts a response an agent can review before sending (so the AI doesn't auto-send to your company email). It's being rolled out to select merchants; the most-requested version is exactly this "AI generates, human reviews, human presses Send" flow for the email channel. Feedback collected this week: the drafted-email **formatting/spacing** needs polish and the suggestions aren't always accurate (e.g. it suggested "in stock" when the item wasn't). If a merchant wants in, log their interest. Until it's GA, keep the AI **off** for company email if every email could get an incorrect auto-reply, and use it on live chat where you can supervise.

### Q21: A customer says they chatted with Chatty but I can't find the conversation in my inbox. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~4 sessions
**Standard answer:**
First check inbox **filters** and all tabs (Open, Resolved, Spam/Automated) and the **date** — anonymous chats show under a random/visitor name, not the customer's order email, so search by date rather than email. If the conversation genuinely never appears (and given the 404/inbox defect this week, see Q7), it may be tied to the inbox loading bug — collect the customer email, approximate date/time, store URL and a screenshot, and escalate so the team can trace it server-side. Don't promise a conversation existed if it can't be located; some "the bot told my customer X" reports turn out to be email threads or other channels.

## 8. Performance / Speed

### Q22: Chatty / my store feels slow since installing it; the AI takes too long to answer. 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~6 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Two different complaints here. (1) **AI response time** — the AI searches your data before replying; 10–30s can happen on larger catalogs or complex queries. If it's consistently very slow (or far slower than during testing), capture the store URL, an example question, browser, and rough timing, and escalate so the team can check. (2) **Store/page speed** — a few merchants attributed storefront slowdown (and lost sales) to the app and asked to cancel/refund. Treat these seriously: confirm whether the slowdown is the storefront widget vs. the admin app, gather the URL and any Shopify/Lighthouse evidence, escalate to TS to investigate the widget's impact, and route the refund request through the refund process (see Q28) — don't dismiss it as unrelated. Logged for product/dev where the AI latency is the issue.

### Q23: Where's the Chatty mobile app, and why am I not getting notifications? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
The Chatty agent app is a **PWA**, not a native App Store/Play Store listing — install it from **app.chatty.net** (open in your phone browser → Add to Home Screen / Install), not by searching the app store (that's why it "won't show in the App Store"). For notifications: agents log in at **app.chatty.net**; on **desktop you must allow browser notifications from Chatty** (Chrome will prompt to Subscribe — accept it) and send a test; on mobile, enable notifications for the installed PWA. If a test notification still doesn't arrive with permissions granted, collect the device/browser and escalate. Online/offline status and alerts also depend on the agent not being in **Away** mode and on the **Live chat** block being enabled.

## 9. Channels — WhatsApp / Messenger / Instagram / Email

### Q24: I want the AI on Messenger/Instagram or WhatsApp — and my WhatsApp number is stuck "Pending" / shows the wrong number. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Connect channels under **Settings → Channels**. To get **AI replies** on a connected channel, also toggle it on under **AI agent → Settings → AI channels** (connecting the channel alone doesn't enable AI). "Pending" on WhatsApp means **Meta verification** is still in progress (usually 24–48h) and is managed on Meta's side; requirements include a Meta Business Manager with **Business Admin** role, completed Business Verification, **2FA** on the number, the number not being live on the personal WhatsApp app or another API provider, and granting all permissions during OAuth. This week a specific blocker recurred: Meta's **Embedded Signup only offered a different/newly-created WABA** (e.g. a US placeholder number) instead of the merchant's existing German WABA — in that case verify the correct **Business Portfolio** is selected and the desired number isn't assigned elsewhere; if the right WABA still can't be chosen, escalate with the **WABA ID, phone number, Business Manager ID, and a screenshot**. Instagram/Messenger replies can also arrive **delayed** (one store saw an 11:12 message answered at 20:51) — flag persistent delays with the page name/ID.

### Q25: My email channel won't verify / a forwarding loop or alias problem / emails missing in Chatty. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~12 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Email connects by **forwarding**: in **Settings → Channels → (your email) → Manage**, copy the Chatty forwarding address (`…@chattyemail.com`) and add a **forwarding rule in your own mail provider** pointing your support inbox to it — verification only completes once forwarding exists on the provider side, and you must click the verification link Chatty sends. Specific cases this week: (a) **aliases** (e.g. `support@` as an alias of a verified `sales@`) often can't be verified separately — connect the real mailbox; (b) **Zoho/GoDaddy/Outlook/Workspace basic plans** may not allow auto-forwarding (the merchant's IT/admin must enable it); (c) a **leftover forwarding rule** can keep sending mail (even bounce loops, e.g. a TikTok bounce to a `.forward@chattyemail.com` address) after you remove the channel — delete the old rule in your provider; (d) if a long-time **"Unverified"** address won't behave, removing and re-adding it then re-running forwarding often fixes it. If forwarding is correctly set and it still won't verify, send a screenshot of the rule and escalate. To enable AI on email, toggle Email under **AI agent → Settings → AI channels** (it auto-replies after ~5 min if no agent responds).

### Q26: Can I use Chatty as an email-only CRM / shared inbox (label, assign, signature) without live chat — and add staff without Shopify's expensive plan? 🆕 new
**Frequency:** ~4 sessions
**Standard answer:**
Yes. You can connect just your **email channel** (Settings → Channels) and skip live chat — the inbox then works as a shared CRM where you reply to customer emails. You can **assign conversations** to team members and **label/tag** them (e.g. "refund," "delayed delivery") for organization. Team members log into Chatty at **app.chatty.net** directly — they do **not** need a Shopify staff seat, so you avoid Shopify's higher plan just to add CS people. Seat counts are by plan (Basic 5, Pro 10, Plus unlimited). Set up an **auto-signature** in settings. Add staff under **Settings → Team** (they accept the invite email and set a password); they can use the desktop PWA or mobile.

## 10. Translation & Language

### Q27: The AI / chatbox replies in the wrong language, or UI labels and AI-drafted emails are still in English. 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~10 sessions
**Standard answer:**
For the AI replying in the wrong language: add a behaviour under **AI agent → Instructions → Manage → General instructions → Behaviours**: "Always respond in the same language as the customer" (and, if needed, limit it to specific languages so it doesn't confuse e.g. Danish with Norwegian). For chatbox **UI labels / navigation text / abandoned-cart popup wording / a stray "now"** still showing English on a non-English store: that's the **Translation** layer — **Settings → Translation**, select the language, **Auto-translate** (or edit the strings), Save, and confirm the status flips from **"Outdated" to "Updated."** When the AI drafts an email on the customer's behalf, it should match the conversation language; if it writes English in a French chat, add the same "respond in the customer's language" behaviour and re-test. Chatbox can also auto-detect the storefront's selected language in many cases — confirm the store's default language is set correctly.

## 11. Pricing, Plans, Cancellation & Refunds

### Q28: How much is Chatty / what's on each plan, and do you offer a discount? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~12 sessions
**Standard answer:**
- **Free** $0 — 50 AI conversations (**lifetime**, not monthly), 100 products, 1 team member, 1 email channel, 90-day history.
- **Basic** $19.99/mo (annual $16.99) — 50 AI conv/mo + $0.40/extra, 500 products, 5 team members, unlimited email channels, 12-month history, full chatbox customization.
- **Pro** $68.99/mo (annual $58.99) — 300 AI conv/mo + $0.40/extra, 8,000 products, 10 team members, full AI channels + analytics, unlimited history.
- **Plus** $199/mo (annual $169.99) — 700 AI conv/mo + $0.40/extra, 20,000 products, unlimited team members, advanced analytics, dedicated AI consultant.
- **Enterprise** — custom.
All paid plans include a **7-day free trial** (the upgrade button shows "Start 7-day free trial"). Confirm the merchant's current plan in Shopify, then map their need (product count, AI volume, seats, languages) to the right tier. **Discounts:** several Free users this week balked at the $0→$20 jump now that Free is 50 lifetime; promotional offers (e.g. a % off for a few months on Pro) are handled case-by-case by the team — collect the request and route to the CS Leader / sales rather than promising a code.

### Q29: How do I cancel / downgrade, and can I get a refund? 🔁 recurring
**Frequency:** ~8 sessions
**Standard answer:**
To stop charges, downgrade to Free: **Chatty → Subscription → View plans → Free plan** (do it before your next billing cycle; your data/settings are kept).
