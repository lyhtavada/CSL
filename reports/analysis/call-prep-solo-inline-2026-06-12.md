---

## 3. ICP Classification

Between **Growing Operator → Scaling CX Lead**: team of 5–20, Klaviyo in the stack, high chat volume.

This group speaks **ROI + integration**, not reassurance. Be specific, demo the real product, tie every feature to a business outcome. Don't open with "is AI real" — they're past that.

---

## 4. Discovery Questions (ask in the call — English)

**Understand the omnichannel need (their stated reason):**
1. When you say "omnichannel," which channels do you want to bring together — email, Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp? Which one brings you the most customer messages today?
2. Outside of web chat, where do customers reach you right now? Are you answering each channel separately in different places?
3. Are you using any other helpdesk alongside Chatty (Gorgias, Zendesk, Shopify Inbox), or is Chatty your main tool?

**Team & operations:**
4. How many people are on your support team, and what hours do they cover?
5. Do you need several teammates answering the same inbox, with assignment and roles?

**Language (a strength to highlight):**
6. Your store runs in French — do customers message you in multiple languages? *(Lead-in to real-time translation.)*

**AI (untapped):**
7. Would you want AI to handle the repeat questions — sizing, shipping, stock — automatically, including after hours?

---

## 5. What to Demo (mapped to their needs)

1. **Unified inbox** — email + Messenger + Instagram + WhatsApp in one place. *This is the "omnichannel" they asked for.*
2. **WhatsApp / Messenger connect flow** — relevant for an international, French-language store.
3. **Real-time Translation (Pro+)** — write in French/English, auto-translate both ways. Strong fit for a multilingual store.
4. **Team seats + assignment** — solves "441 conversations, one person."
5. **AI Assistant** — quick win: train on their 904 products + existing FAQ to auto-answer after hours.

**Closing hook:**
> "You're already handling 441 conversations a month with one person on the Free plan. Pro adds seats, brings every channel into one inbox, and turns on real-time translation for your French and English customers."

---

## 6. Notes & Watch-outs

- **Free plan limits that justify an upgrade:** no real-time translation (Pro+), no extra seats (Free = 1 admin). These are natural reasons to move up — but let them feel the value of omnichannel first; don't lead with price.
- They already pay for Avada SEO Suite + Size Chart — an existing Avada customer, warm relationship.
- Demo in **English** is fine; mention French translation as a feature, not a meeting language, unless they prefer French.
- If they ask about API / security / SLA at depth → that signals Mid-Market, escalate to Liz.
