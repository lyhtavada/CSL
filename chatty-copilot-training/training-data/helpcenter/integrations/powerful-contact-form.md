Q: What is Powerful contact form integration in Chatty?
Q: How do I set up Powerful contact form integration?
Q: How do I connect Powerful contact form integration with Chatty?
A: ### What is Powerful contact form integration?

Powerful contact form integration connects your form app with Chatty inbox, automatically turning each form submission into a conversation.

Get [Powerful contact form](https://apps.shopify.com/form-builder-contact-form?utm_source=chatty) free

### Why integrate Powerful contact form?

With Powerful contact form integration, you can:

- Create individual conversations for each customer who submits a form
- Automatically capture customer details from the form
- Keep all inquiries organized in your inbox
- Respond to customers directly from Chatty
- Track customer conversation history

Forms must include either email or phone number to create conversations.

#### How to set up Powerful contact form integration

#### Set up forwarding email in Chatty

(If you have already connected your email, skip this step.)

Add an email address as a forwarding email in Chatty to receive form submissions.

1. From Chatty, go to **Channels**
2. Click **Add email**
3. Add your email as forwarding email
4. Click **Send verification email** to confirm

#### Copy forwarding email

#### Go to Powerful contact form

#### Open the form you want to connect

#### Paste email in form settings

- Open form builder
- Click **Mail**
- Add the email you set up in Chatty as the form notification email

#### Click Save

#### Manage form conversations

When a customer submits a form, Chatty automatically creates a conversation in your inbox.

Each form submission:

- Creates a new conversation with the customer's profile
- Captures customer information from the form (name, email, phone)
- Shows form content as the first message
- Allows you to reply directly to the customer from Chatty inbox

**Note:** Form notification emails won't create duplicate conversations in your inbox.
