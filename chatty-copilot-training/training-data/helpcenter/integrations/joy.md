Q: How do I connect Joy Rewards & Loyalty Program with Chatty?
A: ### What is Joy Rewards & Loyalty Program?

[Joy](https://apps.shopify.com/joyio?utm_source=Chatty&utm_medium=userguide&utm_campaign=integration) is a Shopify app that provide loyalty program for enhancing customer retention and satisfaction.

It offers a full suite of features (rewards program, points, VIP tiers and referrals…).

Learn more about [Joy Rewards & Loyalty Program](https://apps.shopify.com/joyio?utm_source=Chatty&utm_medium=userguide&utm_campaign=integration)

### Why integrate Joy?

By integrating Chatty with Joy, you can get access to customers’ loyalty profiles and activity right in Chatty Inbox, which includes

- Type
- VIP tier
- Points balance
- Birthday
- Referral link

Then, you have more information to personalize answers to customer inquiries or provide suitable services/products.

You can check Joy profile in:

- Customer details in Contacts
- Conversation details

### How to integrate?

Go to **Integrations**

In "Joy" click **Manage**

#### Install Joy (step 1)

Click **Go to Joy** and install app.

If you have already installed Joy, skip this step

#### Click **Connect** (step 2)

Click Connect to enable loyalty data sync between Joy and Chatty

#### View Joy info

After integrating, you can go to **Customer details** or **Conversation details** to view Joy info
