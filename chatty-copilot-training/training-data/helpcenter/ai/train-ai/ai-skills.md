Q: What is AI skills in Chatty?
Q: How do I set up AI skills?
Q: How does AI skills work with AI assistant?
A: ### What are AI skills

AI skill is a set of specialized capabilities that help your AI assistant handle specific customer scenarios.

Think of skills as training modules that teach your AI how to respond to different types of customer questions, from product recommendations to support requests.

When you enable AI skills, AI assistant can:

- Recommend products based on customer preferences
- Provide accurate size guidance
- Handle common support scenarios automatically
- Transfer conversations to your team when needed

#### Shopping skills

These skills help your AI become an effective sales assistant:

- Smart recommendations
- Size guide
- Inventory status
- Follow-up questions

[Learn more](/ai/train-ai/ai-skills#shopping-skills-1)

#### Customer support skills

These skills help your AI handle common support scenarios automatically:

- When customers ask for human support
- When customers ask about after-sales issues
- When customers ask about order status

[Learn more](/ai/train-ai/ai-skills#customer-support-skills-1)

### Why use AI skills

Without AI skills, your AI might recommend irrelevant products or struggle with specific customer questions.

AI skills solve this by giving your AI focused training on your most important customer scenarios.

This helps you:

- Increase sales by showing customers the right products
- Reduce cart abandonment with confident sizing recommendations
- Save time by automating repetitive support questions

### How to set up AI skills

#### Shopping skills

**Smart recommendations**

You can manage which products AI recommends as best sellers and new arrivals.

Chatty provides two pre-built collections that AI uses for recommendations:

1. **Bestseller:** Products AI suggests when customers ask, "What's popular?" or "What do others buy?"
2. **New arrival**: Products AI suggests when customers ask, "What's new?" or "Any new items?"
3. **Sales promotion:** Products AI suggests when customers ask about "discounts", "deals", or "what's on sale?"
4. **Special occasions:** Products AI suggests when customers ask about "gifts", "birthday presents", or mention special events

**What is smart syncing (auto)?**By default, Chatty automatically does the following for Best sellers and New arrivals:

- Select top 20 products based on sales from the last 30 days
- Update the list daily

You can combine auto-selection with your manual choices by:

- Activate smart sync
- Add/remove manually products from the list

This means: Your recommendation will be updated daily with new products while remaining your manual choices. (maximum 20 products/collection)

**How to set up smart recommendations:**

1. Click **Smart recommendations**
2. Select a collection to manage
3. Set up product list and keywords (custom recommendations)
