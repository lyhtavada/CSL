### How to test AI assistant

AI test zone helps simulate customer interactions before going live, ensuring your AI responds appropriately to customer inquiries.

This helps you verify that the AI's knowledge and responses match your business needs.

#### Before AI assistant test

- [Add your data](/ai/data-sources) (FAQs, product info, etc.)
- [Add custom instruction for AI response](/ai/train-ai) (tone, voice, length of answer)
- [Set up other settings](/ai/settings) (chatbot name, avatar, etc.)

Can I test AI assistant without enabling it?You **can still test AI assistant** with data sources that you added without enabling AI assistant.

#### Go to test zone

Go to **AI assistant** *→*Go to **Test**

#### Chat with your AI

In chatbox, ask and interact with AI assistant as a customer. You can use question examples to ask AI. (Click **Regenerate questions** for more examples)

**Tips for better testing:**

- Test one topic at a time
- Ask the same question in different ways
- Make sure responses aren't too long or too short

#### Check the responses and make improvements

**What to check in AI response:**

- Is the information correct?
- Is the tone friendly but professional?
- Are product details accurate?
- Are prices and availability correct?

You can also review what data sources your AI uses to answer customer questions in test zone.

### How to optimize AI responses

#### A. Unresolved questions

Unresolved questions are customer inquiries that your AI Assistant couldn't answer effectively.

When users click "Talk to a person" ([reply button in AI settings](/ai/overview#how-to-get-your-ai-assistant-ready)), the question is listed as an unresolved question.

We only show unresolved questions in the past 30 days

How to optimize unresolved questions

#### Go to Unresolved questions

#### Check unresolved questions

What you should do with unresolved questions:

1. Look for patterns in unresolved questions to identify gaps in your training data
2. Group similar unresolved questions to create comprehensive answers that address various phrasings of the same inquiry
3. Use unresolved questions to identify new FAQs to add to your help center

#### Add answers to unresolved questions

Select a question from the unresolved list and click **Add answer** →Enter your answer.

Questions and answers will be added to data sources. (in Question list)

#### Test again

Go to **Test zone** and ask the question to ensure that AI assistant can handle that question now.
