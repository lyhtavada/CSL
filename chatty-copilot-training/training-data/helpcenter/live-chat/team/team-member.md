Q: What is **Team member permissions** in Chatty?
Q: How does **Team member permissions** work?
Q: How do I use **Team member permissions** in live chat?
A: - This guide is for team members who are invited to Chatty support team

As a team member invited to Chatty, you’ll collaborate with your team to manage conversations efficiently.

Go to Web app to log in: [https://app.meetchatty.com/](https://app.meetchatty.com/)

### **Team member permissions**

Chatty allows multiple team members to collaborate to manage conversations. Each team member has specific permissions:

- Respond to conversations from all channels (Live chat, Email, Facebook messenger, etc.)
- Access conversation histories and customer's profile
- Leave note
- Resolve, mark as unread and block conversations
- Assign conversations
- Set up notifications and update profile

### Getting started as a team member

#### Accept invitation

Go to your email and check invitation email from Chatty.

Click Create account in email.

#### Create account

Create password for your email to create account to log in Chatty.

Onced logged in, go to **Settings** to edit your name and profile picture.

#### Understand app interface

Your main app interface will be the [Inbox](/live-chat/inbox).

You have access to a list of conversations and your account settings.

#### Set up notifications

You can set up notifications for messages to help you take care of all messages.

Go to **Settings** and set up your notifications on your devices.

Learn more about [Notifications](/others/notifications).

#### Set online/offline status

You can set up online/offline status when you're logging in Chatty.

#### Switch between multiple stores

While logging in Chatty, you can switch between stores to manage conversations from different stores (that your account is linked to).

#### Mention team member in conversation

You and your team can leave private comments within conversations by **Internal note**.
