### Customer's online status

You can check customer's online status on the top banner.

If your customer is offline, message will be sent to their email.

If they are anynomous users (those don't have emails), you'll need to prodive email to keep the conversation going with them.

### Customer behavior

In chat zone, you can check customer behaviour like add to cart or place an order.

List of customer behavior:

- Start a chat
- Add products to empty cart (show with product details)
- Add products to existing cart (show with product details)
- Remove products from cart (show with product details)
- Place an order

### Send message

In chatzone, enter your message and click **Send** or **Enter** to send messages.

You can change the action of **Enter** in settings.

Go to Settings -> General -> Go to Inbox settings -> Select method to send message

### Format text

Make your messages clearer with text formatting: **Bold text, Italic text, Insert link**

You can use icon `B` , `I` or keyboard shortcut. (Ctrl + B, Ctrl + I)

To add link into text in chat, you can:

- Click  and enter your text and link
- Enter your text in chat -> Select text and click  -> Enter your link
- Enter your link in chat -> Click **Edit**

### Real-time translation

Real-time translation detects the customer’s language and lets you translate messages in Inbox.

- In the future, this feature will be limited to usage for all plans.

[Real-time translation](/live-chat/inbox/real-time-translation)

### Insert discount

In chatzone, you can insert and send customer discounts available in your store.

1. In conversation, click
2. Select your discount
3. Click **Send**
4. Discount will be shown in conversation with **Shop now** button. Customer can click S**hop now** to go shopping with your discount.

### Insert FAQs

You can send answers from your [FAQs hub](/build-faqs/add-category) directly in chat.

In conversation, click icon  to get access to FAQs hub. Click to quickly insert your answer and send.

You can only add FAQs in default language.

### Send files

1. In conversation, click icon
2. Select your files. You can send unlimited images and files in chat, maximum 10 files per time.

   Supported image types are: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF. File size must be less than 20MB.
3. Click Send
