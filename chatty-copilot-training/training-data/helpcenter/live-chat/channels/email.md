### Gmail

1. Sign in to your Gmail account
2. Click Settings icon  -> Click **See all settings**
3. Go to "Forward and POP/IMAP" -> Click **Add a forwarding address**
4. Copy forwarding address in instruction -> Paste as forwarding address -> Click **Next**
5. Click **Proceed** to confirm
6. Get back to instruction, click **Get Verification link** -> Follow link and click **Confirm**
7. Go back to Gmail settings -> Go to "Forward and POP/IMAP" -> Select forwarding option **Forward a copy of incoming mail to <email>** -> Click **Save changes**
8. Click **Send verification email** to confirm. Now your email status is verified.

### Outlook

1. Sign in your Outlook account using web browser.

   - If you're using Outlook app, click **File** -> Go to "Info" -> In Account Settings, click link to web.
2. Click Settings icon
3. Go to "Mail" -> "Forwarding and IMAP"
4. Turn on **Enable forwarding**
5. Copy forwarding address in instruction -> Paste as forwarding address -> Click **Save**
6. Click **Send verification email** to confirm. Now your email status is verified.

Follow this interactive guide to connect your Outlook

#### ⚠️ Unable to connect Outlook email to Chatty

When connecting your Outlook email to Chatty, you might see an error message saying your organization doesn't allow external forwarding.

#### Why this happens

- Many organizations disable email forwarding by default for security reasons
- This is common for company/organization Outlook accounts
- The system blocks attempts to forward emails to Chatty's email address

#### How to fix it

1. Contact your organization's IT administrator and take a screenshot of error message
2. Request permission to allow email forwarding to Chatty
3. Go back to Chatty and try connecting your Outlook email again

[Learn more about this with Microsoft.](https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/msoffice/forum/all/550-57520-access-denied-when-forwarding-to-another/c519e969-b4b4-44be-bc40-89509cb4113c)

### Zoho

1. Log in to your Zoho email
2. Click icon Settings
3. Go to **Mail accounts** -> Go to **Forwards**
4. Copy forwarding email in Chatty. Paste it in "Add new email forwarding" and click **Enter**
5. Click **Verify**
6. In step 2 "Verify your email", click **Get confirmation code** -> Copy code and paste in Chatty -> Click **Confirm**
