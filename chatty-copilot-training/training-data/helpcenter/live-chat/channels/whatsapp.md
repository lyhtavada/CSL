A: By connecting WhatsApp, you can check messages from WhatsApp right in Chatty.

### Before connecting WhatsApp to Chatty, make sure you have:

- A Facebook page for your business
- [A WhatsApp Business account linked to that Facebook page](/live-chat/channels/whatsapp#how-to-connect-whatsapp-business-to-a-facebook-page)
- Admin access to both your Facebook page and your WhatsApp Business account

You can connect multiple WhatsApp accounts to Chatty.

### How to connect WhatsApp to Chatty

#### Go to channels settings

1. Go to **Channels** in your Chatty dashboard
2. Find the WhatsApp section
3. Click **Manage**

#### Connect your Facebook account

1. Click **Connect account**
2. You'll be redirected to Facebook login
3. Enter your Facebook credentials and log in
4. Review the permissions that Chatty requests
5. Click **Continue** to grant access

#### Select your WhatsApp account

1. After connecting Facebook, you'll see a list of WhatsApp Business accounts linked to your Facebook page
2. Select the WhatsApp account you want to connect to Chatty
3. Click **Connect** to complete the setup

#### Confirm connection

1. You'll see a confirmation message that WhatsApp is connected
2. WhatsApp conversations will now appear in your Chatty inbox with a WhatsApp icon
3. Test the connection by sending a message to your WhatsApp Business number

### How to connect WhatsApp Business to a Facebook page

#### Download WhatsApp Business app

#### Login Facebook

1. From the Tools tab, tap Facebook & Instagram
2. Click Facebook
3. Click Continue
4. Log in

#### Connect Facebook page

1. Select the Facebook Page you want to link to your WhatsApp Business account
2. Click Connect WhatsApp

If you don’t have a Facebook Page, you’ll have the option to create one

#### Successfully connected WhatsApp

You’ll now see a WhatsApp button on your Facebook page.

Once you return to the WhatsApp Business app, you’ll see your linked Facebook Page at the top of the Facebook & Instagram screen.

### WhatsApp template

WhatsApp templates are pre-approved message formats that let you start conversations with customers. They're the only way to send WhatsApp messages to customers who:

- Haven't messaged you before
- Haven't sent you a message in the last 24 hours

Templates come in three types:

- **Marketing**: Promotional offers, product announcements, sales campaigns
- **Utility**: Order updates, shipping notifications, appointment reminders
- **Authentication (not supported)**: Verification codes, security messages

Once a customer replies to your template message, you can have a normal conversation for the next 24 hours without needing templates.
