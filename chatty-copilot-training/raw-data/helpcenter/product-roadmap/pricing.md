chevron-rightHow much do the paid plans cost?[hashtag](#how-much-do-the-paid-plans-cost)

We have Basic monthly plan at $19.99; Pro at $68.99; Plus at $199.
See plan comparisons for more details.

chevron-rightWhat additional features do the paid plans offer?[hashtag](#what-additional-features-do-the-paid-plans-offer)

We have some new features help you better support customers:

- Team management
- Auto translate feature

chevron-rightWill there be a trial period for the paid features?[hashtag](#will-there-be-a-trial-period-for-the-paid-features)

We offer a 7-day free trial for any paid plan. You can cancel anytime during the trial period.

chevron-rightWill I lose access to the features I'm currently using for free? If no, what are free features?[hashtag](#will-i-lose-access-to-the-features-im-currently-using-for-free-if-no-what-are-free-features)

These following features will be limited in Chatty free version:

- Customize chatbox
- Auto translation
- Chat history: 90 days
- No follow-up email
- Chat avatar customization

chevron-rightAre there any discounts for long-term subscriptions?[hashtag](#are-there-any-discounts-for-long-term-subscriptions)

Yes. To celebrate our launch, we're offering Early bird deal: 50% off for the first 3 months

chevron-rightHow to apply a discount code?[hashtag](#how-to-apply-a-discount-code)

Go to Subscription *→* In **Discount code**, insert your code and click **Apply**.

chevron-rightWhat happens to my existing data and settings if I don't upgrade?[hashtag](#what-happens-to-my-existing-data-and-settings-if-i-dont-upgrade)

Your settings and data will be safe.

chevron-rightWill my chat history be affected if I don't upgrade?[hashtag](#will-my-chat-history-be-affected-if-i-dont-upgrade)

Your chat history will be removed if it more than 90 days.

chevron-rightCan I have a refund?[hashtag](#can-i-have-a-refund)

Yes, you can. We offer a 30-day Money Back Guarantee (30 days from the start of your free trial), which applies for the following circumstances:

- You do not wish to continue the subscription after the first month
- You cancel the paid plan before the first circle ends
- You forget cancel trial period or have accidentally choose the paid plan without using it in the first month

chevron-rightWhat happens if I exceed the monthly AI conversation limit?[hashtag](#what-happens-if-i-exceed-the-monthly-ai-conversation-limit)

- Free plan users: AI assistant will be deactivated. You can still handle conversations manually in Inbox.
- Paid plan users: Additional AI conversations will be automatically charged according to your plan’s overage rates.

chevron-rightWhat happens if my store exceeds the product limit?[hashtag](#what-happens-if-my-store-exceeds-the-product-limit)

If your store exceeds the product limit for your plan, only the most recently added products will be used by the AI assistant.

You can upgrade to a higher plan with an increased product limit.
