### [hashtag](#two-step-verification-for-new-devices) Two-step verification for new devices

When you log in to Chatty from a new device or browser, we verify your identity using two-step verification to protect your account security.

chevron-rightWhy use two-step verification[hashtag](#why-use-two-step-verification)

Two-step verification adds an extra layer of security to your Chatty account. Even if someone has your password, they cannot access your account without access to your email for the verification code.

#### [hashtag](#how-it-works) How it works

1. Log in with your email and password
2. A verification code will be sent to your registered email address
3. Enter the verification code to complete your login
4. Once verified, you can access your Chatty dashboard on the new device

If you don't receive the verification code:

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Click "Resend code" on the verification screen
- Make sure you're using the email address registered with your Chatty account
