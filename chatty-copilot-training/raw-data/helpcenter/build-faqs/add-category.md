### [hashtag](#what-is-category) What is category?

Category helps grouping questions in the same topic.

In default, we have 2 categories (with questions) which are popular for Shopify users:

1. Order & Shipping
2. Exchange & Return

You can edit, add more questions and delete categories.

#### [hashtag](#enter-information-for-category) Enter information for category

- **Category name:** Title of Category displayed in the FAQ page
- **Icon:** Select a relevant icon that helps users understand your content. We also support some other icons. Click **View more** to check out other options. You can also upload your own icons by clicking icon upload.
- **Position**: Position of the Category in the FAQ page from top to bottom
- **Status: Keep the category ublished or draft**
- **Feature category:** This helps you to list the category in the first page chatbox.
