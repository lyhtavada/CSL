### [hashtag](#what-is-embedded-chatbox) What is embedded chatbox?

Embedded chatbox lets you add your complete chatbox directly into any page on your store as a full-width section.

Unlike the floating chatbox button, this embeds the entire chat interface seamlessly into your page layout.

You can embed the full chatbox experience directly into specific pages like:

- Dedicated support or contact pages
- Product information pages
- FAQ pages with live support access

You can use both embedded and floating chatbox together. For example, have floating chat available everywhere, plus embedded one on your contact page.

### [hashtag](#how-to-add-embedded-chatbox-to-your-page) How to add embedded chatbox to your page

1

**Go to Chatbox**

2

In the **Advanced** tab, find **Embedded chatbox**

3

Click **Go to theme**

4

You'll be redirected to your theme editor. **Choose where to add chatbox.**

5

Click **Add section**

6

Click **Save**
