chevron-rightWhy can't guest automatically convert to customer?[hashtag](#why-cant-guest-automatically-convert-to-customer)

Chatty doesn't automatically convert guest to customer to maintain data accuracy and protect your customers' experience:

- **Prevents duplicate or inaccurate customer profiles**: Automatic conversion could create multiple profiles for the same customer or generate profiles with incomplete information.
- **Protects customer login access**: Creating customer accounts without verification could prevent customers from accessing their actual accounts later.

**What you can do instead:**

1. Review guest information manually before creating customer profiles
2. Create customer profiles with validated information

### [hashtag](#manage-customers) Manage customers

#### [hashtag](#block-customers) Block customers

You can block a customer so that you do not get any messages from that customer.

In their conversation, in the right panel, click **Block user** and confirm.

Customers who get blocked won't be notified that they're block. They can still message you in the chatbox, but they cannot receive replies.

#### [hashtag](#delete-customers) Delete customers

You can delete customers in Contacts, where you can manage customer information.
