1

#### [hashtag](#confirm-connection) Confirm connection

1. You'll see a confirmation message that WhatsApp is connected

### [hashtag](#whatsapp-template) WhatsApp template

WhatsApp templates are pre-approved message formats that let you start conversations with customers. They're the only way to send WhatsApp messages to customers who:

- Haven't messaged you before
- Haven't sent you a message in the last 24 hours

Templates come in three types:

- **Marketing**: Promotional offers, product announcements, sales campaigns
- **Utility**: Order updates, shipping notifications, appointment reminders
- **Authentication (not supported)**: Verification codes, security messages

Once a customer replies to your template message, you can have a normal conversation for the next 24 hours without needing templates.

### [hashtag](#faqs) FAQs

chevron-rightI don't see any WhatsApp accounts when trying to connect. What should I do?[hashtag](#i-dont-see-any-whatsapp-accounts-when-trying-to-connect.-what-should-i-do)

Your WhatsApp Business account must be connected to your Facebook page first. Follow these steps:

1. Go to your Facebook page settings
2. Navigate to WhatsApp
3. Connect your WhatsApp Business account to your Facebook page
4. Return to Chatty and try connecting again

chevron-rightCan I connect my personal WhatsApp number?[hashtag](#can-i-connect-my-personal-whatsapp-number)

No, you can only connect WhatsApp Business accounts. Personal WhatsApp accounts cannot be integrated with business tools like Chatty.

chevron-rightWhat if I don't have a WhatsApp Business account?[hashtag](#what-if-i-dont-have-a-whatsapp-business-account)

You'll need to create a WhatsApp Business account first:

1. Download WhatsApp Business app
2. Set up your business profile
3. Connect it to your Facebook page
4. Make sure Meta approves your account
5. Return to Chatty to complete the integration

Read [this guidearrow-up-right](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/2087193751603668?id=2129163877102343) for more details.

chevron-rightWhy is my WhatsApp Business number showing as "Pending"?[hashtag](#why-is-my-whatsapp-business-number-showing-as-pending)

When your WhatsApp Business number shows "Pending" status in Chatty, it means Meta (WhatsApp) hasn't approved your number yet, even though you've completed the connection process.

There are two main reasons why your WhatsApp Business number remains pending:

#### [hashtag](#two-factor-authentication-2fa-not-enabled) Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) not enabled

1. Open your WhatsApp Business app
2. Go to Settings → Account → Two-step verification
3. Turn on Two-step verification and set up a 6-digit PIN
4. Wait for Meta to approve your number (Meta's approval process typically takes 24-48 hours, but can take longer during busy periods.**)**
5. Reconnect to Chatty once approved

#### [hashtag](#display-name-doesnt-meet-metas-guidelines) Display name doesn't meet Meta's guidelines

Learn more about [WhatsApp Business display namearrow-up-right](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/338047025165344)

chevron-rightCan I create a new WhatsApp account during the connection process?[hashtag](#can-i-create-a-new-whatsapp-account-during-the-connection-process)

Yes, if you don't have a WhatsApp Business account connected to your Facebook page, you can create one during the setup process. Note that this will be a new WhatsApp number, not linked to any existing personal WhatsApp account.

chevron-rightCan I disconnect WhatsApp later?[hashtag](#can-i-disconnect-whatsapp-later)

Yes, you can disconnect WhatsApp at any time:

1. Go to Channels → WhatsApp → Manage
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm the disconnection Existing conversation history will remain in Chatty but no new messages will sync.

chevron-rightWill customers know I'm using Chatty to reply?[hashtag](#will-customers-know-im-using-chatty-to-reply)

No, customers will see your responses as coming directly from your WhatsApp Business account. The integration is invisible to customers.
