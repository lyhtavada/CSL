### [hashtag](#gmail) Gmail

1. Sign in to your Gmail account
2. Click Settings icon  -> Click **See all settings**
3. Go to "Forward and POP/IMAP" -> Click **Add a forwarding address**
4. Copy forwarding address in instruction -> Paste as forwarding address -> Click **Next**
5. Click **Proceed** to confirm
6. Get back to instruction, click **Get Verification link** -> Follow link and click **Confirm**
7. Go back to Gmail settings -> Go to "Forward and POP/IMAP" -> Select forwarding option **Forward a copy of incoming mail to <email>** -> Click **Save changes**
8. Click **Send verification email** to confirm. Now your email status is verified.

Follow this interactive guide to connect your Gmail

### [hashtag](#outlook) Outlook

1. Sign in your Outlook account using web browser.

   - If you're using Outlook app, click **File** -> Go to "Info" -> In Account Settings, click link to web.
2. Click Settings icon
3. Go to "Mail" -> "Forwarding and IMAP"
4. Turn on **Enable forwarding**
5. Copy forwarding address in instruction -> Paste as forwarding address -> Click **Save**
6. Click **Send verification email** to confirm. Now your email status is verified.

Follow this interactive guide to connect your Outlook

#### [hashtag](#unable-to-connect-outlook-email-to-chatty) ⚠️ Unable to connect Outlook email to Chatty

When connecting your Outlook email to Chatty, you might see an error message saying your organization doesn't allow external forwarding.

#### [hashtag](#why-this-happens) Why this happens

- Many organizations disable email forwarding by default for security reasons
- This is common for company/organization Outlook accounts
- The system blocks attempts to forward emails to Chatty's email address

#### [hashtag](#how-to-fix-it) How to fix it

1. Contact your organization's IT administrator and take a screenshot of error message
2. Request permission to allow email forwarding to Chatty
3. Go back to Chatty and try connecting your Outlook email again

### [hashtag](#zoho) Zoho

1. Log in to your Zoho email
2. Click icon Settings
3. Go to **Mail accounts** -> Go to **Forwards**
4. Copy forwarding email in Chatty. Paste it in "Add new email forwarding" and click **Enter**
5. Click **Verify**
6. In step 2 "Verify your email", click **Get confirmation code** -> Copy code and paste in Chatty -> Click **Confirm**
7. Click **Send verification email** to confirm. Now your email status is verified.

### [hashtag](#hostinger) Hostinger

1. Sign in your Hostinger email
2. Click Manage
3. Go to **Forwarders** -> Click **Create a forwarder**
4. Copy forwarding email in Chatty. Paste it in "To email address" and click Create
5. Click **Send verification email** to confirm. Now your email status is verified.

### [hashtag](#faqs) FAQs

chevron-rightCan I use different types of email providers?[hashtag](#can-i-use-different-types-of-email-providers)

Yes, Chatty works with most email providers including Gmail, Outlook, and custom domain emails. The verification process remains the same regardless of your provider.

chevron-rightI'm trying to add an alias email, but I don't receive the verification link. What should I do?[hashtag](#im-trying-to-add-an-alias-email-but-i-dont-receive-the-verification-link.-what-should-i-do)

When you add an alias email, the verification link is sent to your primary (main) email address, not to the alias itself.

How to add alias emails:

1. **First, verify your primary email address**

   - Add your main email address to Chatty
   - Check your inbox for the verification link
   - Click the link to verify
2. **Then, add your alias emails**

   - Go to Channels and add your alias email
   - When prompted for verification, you can skip to the final step
   - Click "Verify" to complete the setup
   - You won't need to check your inbox again since the primary email is already verified

**Example scenario:**

- Email A ([[email protected]envelope](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#543d3a323b142d3b2126303b39353d3a7a373b39)) is your primary email - verify this first
- Email B ([[email protected]envelope](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#592a2c2929362b2d1920362c2b3d3634383037773a3634)) is an alias of Email A - skip inbox verification

All emails sent to either address will forward to your Chatty inbox.

chevron-rightWhat if I only want to connect the alias email, not the primary one?[hashtag](#what-if-i-only-want-to-connect-the-alias-email-not-the-primary-one)

You can do this, but you'll still need to verify the primary email first:

1. Add and verify your primary email
2. Add your alias email (skip inbox verification)
3. Once the alias is connected, remove the primary email from Channels

Your alias email will continue working in Chatty.
