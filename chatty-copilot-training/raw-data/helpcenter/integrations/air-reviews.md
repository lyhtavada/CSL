### [hashtag](#why-chatty-for-reviews-collection) Why Chatty for reviews collection

Chatty is an AI-powered live chat platform that helps you engage with customers in real-time to solve their concerns. Then Chatty converts these positive interactions into reviews.

By using Chatty with Air Reviews, you can create a complete feedback loop that turns conversations into social proof for your store.

Chatty helps:

- Capture customer's situation in real-time before it becomes a public review
- Resolve issues quickly to prevent negative reviews
- Create personalized touchpoints for requesting reviews
- Provide 24/7 support with AI to address customer questions instantly

#### [hashtag](#respond-quickly-to-prevent-negative-reviews) Respond quickly to prevent negative reviews

When customers have issues, the speed of your response can determine whether they leave a negative review or become loyal advocates:

1. Set up [notifications](/others/notifications) in Chatty to alert you of new messages
2. Use [quick replies](/live-chat/quick-replies) for common questions
3. Prioritize responding to customers who express frustration
4. Resolve issues completely before requesting a review
