### [hashtag](#why-integrate-joy) Why integrate Joy?

By integrating Chatty with Joy, you can get access to customers’ loyalty profiles and activity right in Chatty Inbox, which includes

- Type
- VIP tier
- Points balance
- Birthday
- Referral link

Then, you have more information to personalize answers to customer inquiries or provide suitable services/products.

You can check Joy profile in:

- Customer details in Contacts
- Conversation details

### [hashtag](#how-to-integrate) How to integrate?

1

Go to **Integrations**

2

In "Joy" click **Manage**

3

#### [hashtag](#install-joy-step-1) Install Joy (step 1)

Click **Go to Joy** and install app.

If you have already installed Joy, skip this step

4

#### [hashtag](#click-connect-step-2) Click **Connect** (step 2)

Click Connect to enable loyalty data sync between Joy and Chatty

5

#### [hashtag](#view-joy-info) View Joy info

After integrating, you can go to **Customer details** or **Conversation details** to view Joy info
