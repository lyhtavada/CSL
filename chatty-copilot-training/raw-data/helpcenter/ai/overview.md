### [hashtag](#what-is-ai-assistant) What is AI assistant?

Al assistant helps you solve customers' questions faster and 24/7. AI assistant is trained on your data.

This means:

- faster resolution times
- happier customers
- less strain on your support team

This empowers your customer support by maintaining your brand voice throughout all conversations 24/7 (even if you are a solo entrepreneur).

#### [hashtag](#when-using-ai-assistant) When using AI assistant

- All conversations will be answered automatically by AI assistant. In conversation detail, you will see AI assistant as an assignee.
- During the conversation handled by AI assistant, you can always join in the conversation to chat by clicking **Join conversation**.
- **Chat summary by AI:** When the conversation is transferred to your team, AI will summarize the whole conversation.
  This helps you quickly get the context and deliver faster customer service without reading the entire chat history.
  AI will detect language, highlight main issues, and provide helpful suggestions for your response.
