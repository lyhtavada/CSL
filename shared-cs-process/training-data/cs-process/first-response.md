# First Response Templates

## General Greeting

> Hi [Customer's Name], this is [Your Name] from Avada Support. Thanks for reaching out — I'm happy to assist you today!

## Greeting + Empathy (for issues)

> Hi [Customer's Name]! Thanks for contacting us. I'm sorry to hear you're running into this issue — let me look into it right away for you.

## Greeting + Pre-sales

> Hi [Customer's Name]! Thanks for your interest in [Product]. I'd be happy to answer your questions and help you find the right plan for your store.

## Greeting + Complaint

> Hi [Customer's Name], thank you for reaching out. I'm really sorry for the experience you've had — I completely understand your frustration. Let me look into this immediately and get it sorted for you.

## Greeting + Returning Customer

> Hi [Customer's Name], welcome back! How can I help you today?

## Setting Expectations (need time to check)

> Let me check this for you right away. It might take about 2–3 minutes — thanks for your patience!

## Setting Expectations (need to escalate)

> I've looked into this and I'd like to have our technical team take a closer look to make sure we get this right for you. I'll create a ticket for them now and keep you updated on the progress.
