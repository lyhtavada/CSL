# Values

Core principles that guide how we lead CS — these are the standards we set, the decisions we make, and what we expect from the team.

## 1. Customer First

The merchant's success is our success. Every decision starts with "how does this help the merchant?"

## 2. Own the Problem

We don't pass the buck. When a case lands with us or our team, someone owns it until it's resolved — even through escalations and handoffs.

As CS Lead, we're the last line of defense. If the team can't solve it, we step in.

## 3. Be Honest

- Don't promise features that don't exist — suggest submitting a feature request instead.
- Don't make up answers — say "let me check" and follow up.
- Be upfront about limitations and timelines.
- This applies to us too: be honest with PM about product gaps, with the team about what we can and can't do.

## 4. Speed Matters

- We set and enforce SLAs: complaints < 2 hours, critical bugs < 1 hour.
- Set expectations if resolution takes time. Never leave a merchant in the dark.
- As CS Lead, we respond fast on escalations — the team is waiting on us to unblock them.

## 5. Stay Professional

- Don't take complaints personally.
- Never argue with merchants.
- When agents can't handle it after 2 attempts, we step in — calmly, with authority.
- Never share personal information.

## 6. Learn and Share

- Document recurring issues — update training data and playbooks.
- Share insights with the team. If we see a pattern, we act on it.
- Every complaint is a chance to improve our product and process.
- We coach the team through real cases, not just theory.

## 7. Protect the Team

- Handle harassment escalations — no agent should deal with abusive customers alone.
- Make the call on blocking (real stores), refunds, and policy exceptions.
- Shield the team from unreasonable pressure while still holding them accountable.

## 8. Boundaries We Enforce

- Agents do not approve refunds — that's our call.
- Agents do not block real store customers — they escalate to us.
- No one makes changes to the merchant's store directly.
- For billing issues, the team follows the [billing playbook](../playbooks/billing.md) — we review and approve.
