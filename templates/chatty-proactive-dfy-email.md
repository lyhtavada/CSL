# Chatty Proactive DFY — Email Template

Email CS/TS gửi KH mới cài Pro/Plus sau khi audit store. KHÔNG gửi bản chung chung — phải điền phần `[...]` bằng đúng cái thấy được từ audit của store đó. Thay `[Tên CS]` bằng tên người gửi.

Tone: theo `_identity/tone-and-voice.md` — mở đầu "Hi there, This is ... from Avada Support Team", KHÔNG bold, KHÔNG em dash (—). Friendly, đồng hành, một CTA rõ.

---

## Template A — Store mới cài, widget còn default / chưa setup

> **Subject:** Your free Chatty setup is ready, [Store name]
>
> Hi there,
>
> This is [Tên CS] from Avada Support Team. I look after merchants who just started with Chatty, and I had a look at your store to see how things are going.
>
> I noticed [điền từ audit: your chat widget is still on the default look / you have not added any FAQs yet / your AI assistant has not been trained on your products yet]. That is completely normal at this stage, and it is exactly the kind of thing I can take care of for you.
>
> We offer a free done-for-you setup where I personally set Chatty up to fit your store: your branding on the chat widget, a starter set of FAQs, and the AI assistant trained on your products and pages. Everything built around your store and your customers, not a generic template.
>
> You do not need to do anything on your end. Just reply to this email and I will get started, then show you the first result for your review.
>
> Looking forward to helping you get the most out of Chatty.
>
> Best,
> [Tên CS]
> Avada Support Team

## Cách dùng
1. Audit store trước (theo SOP `playbooks/chatty-proactive-dfy-pilot.md` Bước 1 + tool audit).
2. Chọn template theo tình huống: A (mới cài chưa setup) / B (đang trial sắp hết) / C (đã trả tiền, usage thấp).
3. Điền các `[...]` bằng đúng cái thấy được từ audit — cụ thể, không chung chung.
4. Thay `[Store name]` / `[Pro/Plus]` cho đúng.
5. Gửi + tạo DFY ticket ngay (tag `DFY-new` + `proactive`), lưu nội dung email vào ticket.

> Nếu KH reply "yes" → vào flow làm DFY theo `playbooks/chatty-dfy-flow.md` (M1 → M2 → M3).
