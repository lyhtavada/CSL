# Follow-up Templates

## Issue Resolved — Confirmation

> Great news — the issue has been resolved! Could you please check on your end and let me know if everything looks good now?

## Issue Resolved — Farewell + Review Ask

> Glad we got that sorted for you! If you're happy with the support, we would truly appreciate it if you could leave us a quick review — it really helps our team continue improving. Is there anything else I can help with?

## Bug Escalated — Waiting for TS

> I've escalated this to our technical team for a deeper look. They'll investigate and I'll update you as soon as I hear back. Thank you for your patience!

## Bug Fix Deployed — Follow-up

> Hi [Customer's Name]! Just following up — our technical team has deployed a fix for the issue you reported. Could you please check if everything is working correctly on your end now?

## Refund in Progress

> I've submitted your refund request to our team. Refunds typically take 7–10 business days to appear in your account. I'll follow up once it's confirmed on our end.

## Feature Request Acknowledged

> Thank you for the suggestion! I've passed this along to our product team. While I can't guarantee a timeline, your feedback is really valuable and helps shape our roadmap.

## No Response Follow-up (after 24h)

> Hi [Customer's Name], just checking in — were you able to resolve the issue? Let me know if you need any further help. I'm happy to assist!

## Closing Inactive Conversation

> Hi [Customer's Name], since I haven't heard back, I'll go ahead and close this conversation for now. Feel free to reach out anytime if you need help in the future!

## Achieve More — Feature Suggestion

> By the way, I noticed you haven't activated [Feature X] yet — it could really help with [goal]. Would you like me to walk you through setting it up?
