# Template: No Retroactive Credit for Past Orders

**Use when:** Merchant requests a refund/credit for orders that were counted in past billing cycles, but a fix has since been applied going forward.

---

Hi there,

This is Liz from Joy Support Team. I'm following up on your request regarding [**the orders that were counted during [time period]**] and the possibility of a credit.

We've taken time to review this carefully with our product and development teams. During the period you mentioned, [**those orders**] were still received and processed by our system as standard Shopify orders. From a technical perspective, this means our system continued to handle those orders, apply internal logic, and consume infrastructure resources as usual. Because of this, we're unable to offer a retroactive credit for the orders processed in the past.

That said, as shared earlier, our team has now updated the logic to [**exclude [order type] from the order count**]. Going forward, [**those orders**] will no longer be included in your usage calculation, so you will not be charged for them.

We truly appreciate your understanding and thank you for raising this with us. If you notice anything unexpected after monitoring upcoming orders, or if you have further questions, please feel free to reach out. We're always here to help.

Best regards,
Liz
Joy Support Team

- `[the orders that were counted during [time period]]` → mô tả loại order + khoảng thời gian (e.g. "TikTok orders counted over the past 3 months")
- `[those orders]` → tên loại order (e.g. "TikTok orders", "POS orders")
