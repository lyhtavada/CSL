# POS, Integrations, Settings

## POS (Advanced+ only)
In-store loyalty via Shopify POS.

**Requirements**:
- Joy installed on POS device
- Cart has customer + products
- Customer has account balance to redeem

**Customer management**: Always verify customer identity first (email/phone). Search before creating — creating without identifying creates duplicate customers on Shopify.

**Earning**: Automatic on POS orders (same as online).
**Redeeming**: At POS checkout, customer must have existing balance.

## Integrations
- **Product Reviews**: Klaviyo Reviews, Judge.me, Yotpo, Fera, Air Reviews, Review Rocket
- **Chat Apps**: Chatty, Gorgias — show loyalty data in conversations
- **Email/SMS**: Klaviyo, Omnisend, Mailchimp, Sendlane, Drip, PushOwl
- **Subscription**: Joy Subscription, Shopify Subscription, Recharge
- **Shopify Flow**: Custom automations (Loox, Okendo, Stamped, Zigpoll, Growave)

## Notifications
Automated emails for loyalty events (points earned, tier change, birthday, etc.).
Shopify Flow triggers for custom workflows.

## Analytics (All plans)
Dashboard metrics:
- Revenue by Joy (from discount redemptions)
- Loyalty Members (total enrolled)
- Referred Revenue
- Redemption Rate (% redeeming over time)
- Points Earned / Points Redeemed

Drill-down: loyalty member insights, engagement levels (tier distribution, at-risk/dormant), referral performance, point activity.
