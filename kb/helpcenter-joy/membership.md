# Membership

## Member Exclusive Deal
Marketing tool showing special benefits on product pages to loyalty members. Non-members see what they could unlock by joining.

**Setup**:
1. Joy dashboard > Membership > Member exclusive deal > Setup
2. Set active dates, discount type, order requirements, eligible products, discount combinations
3. Customize design: action text, CTA buttons, font, icons, colors
4. Add "Joy: Exclusive Deal" app block in theme editor

Appears at bottom of product pages by default (repositionable).

## VIP Tiers (Advanced+ only)
Multi-level loyalty with automatic progression.

**Calculation methods**:
- Points earned (encourages diverse engagement)
- Amount spent (rewards high spenders)
- Number of orders (incentivizes frequency)

**Per tier**:
- Entry Reward: one-time on reaching tier (discount, bonus points, free product, free shipping, BOGO)
- Tier Privileges (Perks): ongoing benefits on all orders while in tier
- Tier Assessment: auto upgrade/downgrade based on rules

**Demotion**: Auto tier reset or downgrade after inactivity.

**Design**: Custom icons, card colors, background styling.

Important: Do NOT add entry rewards to lowest tier — may issue massive rewards to unwanted customers.

Note: Shopify allows max 25 automatic discounts across all tiers combined.
