# Customers & Activities

## Customer Management
- Import customer data via CSV
- Manage customer types and segments
- Generate QR codes and Apple Wallet passes
- Exclude specific customers or B2B customers from program
- Manual point adjustments
- Birthday collection via registration form

## Activities Dashboard
Three tracking areas:
- **Referral Activities**: Referee status, referral earnings
- **Review Activities**: Approved reviews, reward points
- **Google Review Submission**: Google reviews and rewards

**Point Activities**: Filter by status (Earned, Redeemed, Refunded, Expired). Adjust points directly.

**Customer Management** (from sidebar): Redeem points for rewards, remove from program, update info (name, birthday, VIP tier).

**Referral Tracking**: View all referral activities. Can cancel referrals (voids referee discount code, halts new referral code generation).
