# AM Call Talk Track — Chatty

> **Dùng cho:** Andy, Jade
> **Mục đích:** Onboarding call + support call với ICP Chatty merchants
> **Nguyên tắc:** Đây là talk track, không phải script cứng. Hiểu flow và intent — không đọc y chang.

---

## Before the Call (5 min prep)

Trước khi gọi, mở merchant profile và check nhanh:

- [ ] Họ đang dùng plan gì? (Free / Basic / Pro / Plus)
- [ ] Đã setup AI chưa? (check training data sources có gì)
- [ ] Chatbox đang on hay off?
- [ ] Đã có conversation nào chưa?
- [ ] Ngày cài: bao lâu rồi mà chưa setup?
- [ ] Tên người liên hệ là ai? (Owner / eCommerce Manager / Ops Lead?)

**Mục tiêu call này là gì?**
- Onboarding: dẫn merchant qua setup, ra khỏi call với AI đang chạy
- Support: unblock 1 vấn đề cụ thể, confirm merchant hiểu cách dùng

---

## Call Flow

### 1. Opening (1–2 min)

**Mục tiêu:** Tạo tone friendly, confirm mục đích, lấy permission tiếp tục.

> "Hi [Name], this is [your name] from the Chatty support team — how are you doing today?"

*(chờ họ trả lời — đừng lao vào ngay)*

> "I'm reaching out because I noticed you recently [installed Chatty / upgraded to [plan]] — I wanted to check in and make sure everything's working well for you. Do you have about 15–20 minutes?"

**Nếu họ bận:**
> "No problem at all — is there a better time I can reach you? I can send a quick email with my availability."

---

### 2. Discovery (3–5 min)

**Mục tiêu:** Hiểu tình trạng hiện tại, pain point, kỳ vọng. Nghe nhiều hơn nói.

**Câu mở:**
> "Before I walk you through anything, I'd love to hear from you — what made you install Chatty? What are you hoping it'll help with?"

**Follow-up questions (chọn theo context):**

| Tình huống | Câu hỏi |
|-----------|---------|
| Chưa biết họ dùng AI chưa | "Have you had a chance to set up the AI assistant yet?" |
| Có vẻ overwhelmed | "How has the setup experience been so far — anything feeling confusing or unclear?" |
| Dùng rồi nhưng chưa thấy kết quả | "Have you seen any conversations coming through yet — how did the AI handle them?" |
| Đang dùng tool khác | "What were you using before Chatty? What made you switch / want to try something new?" |

**Red flags — note lại:**
- Kỳ vọng AI xử lý 100% mà không cần người → set expectation ngay
- Store chưa có traffic → AI sẽ chưa prove value ngay
- Không biết CS gap của mình là gì → cần educate thêm

---

### 3. Setup Walkthrough (5–10 min)

**Dùng cho onboarding call. Skip nếu họ đã setup xong.**

**Mục tiêu:** Ra khỏi call với chatbox on + AI đang nhận data.

#### Bước 1: Chatbox on
> "First thing — let's make sure your chatbox is live on your store. Can you open your Shopify theme editor real quick?"

- App embed on?
- Chatbox block showing on storefront?
- Nếu không hiện: check display rules → có đang exclude homepage không?

#### Bước 2: AI data sources
> "The AI learns from your store data — the more you give it, the better it answers. Let's check what it's already pulling in."

- Products synced? (chỉ published + in-stock)
- Policies added? (return, shipping)
- FAQs in Chatty? → AI sẽ dùng làm training data
- Custom Q&A: "Is there anything your customers ask a lot that the AI might not know from products alone?"

#### Bước 3: Test AI
> "Let's do a quick test — I'll ask your AI a question as if I'm a customer."

- Mở Test zone trong Chatty
- Hỏi 1 product question cụ thể về store của họ
- Nếu AI trả lời tốt: "Great — this is already working well for you."
- Nếu AI sai/không biết: "This is fixable — let me show you how to add a Q&A to train it."

---

### 4. Value Moment (2–3 min)

**Mục tiêu:** Kết nối setup với outcome họ care — đừng nói feature, nói result.

> "Once your AI is trained on your products and FAQs, it handles customer questions 24/7 — so even when your team is offline, customers still get answers and can make purchase decisions."

**Nếu họ mentioned pain point cụ thể, tie back:**

| Pain họ nói | Value frame |
|------------|------------|
| "Miss messages overnight" | "That's exactly what AI covers — it's live even at 2 AM." |
| "Team overwhelmed with repetitive questions" | "AI can handle the repeat questions so your team focuses on the complex ones." |
| "Spending on ads but conversion is low" | "If visitors can't get answers, they leave. AI answers instantly — that's where the conversion lift comes from." |
| "Used [X] before but AI was bad" | "The difference here is AI learns from your actual products — so it knows your store, not just generic answers." |

---

### 5. Escalation Scope & Human Handoff (1–2 min)

> "One thing to know — AI is smart but it knows its limits. When a customer asks something complex, or wants to talk to a human, AI will transfer the conversation to your team. You'll get a notification, and you'll see the full chat history so you don't have to ask them to repeat anything."

- Confirm they know how to pick up a transferred conversation
- Ask: "Do you have someone who monitors the inbox during business hours?"

---

### 6. Next Steps + Close (1–2 min)

**Onboarding call:**
> "So by end of today, your AI should be live and answering product questions. Over the next few days, just monitor the conversations — if you see anything AI gets wrong, drop me a message and I'll help you fix the training data. Sound good?"

**Support call:**
> "We've unblocked [issue]. Is there anything else you want to cover before I let you go?"

**Always close with:**
> "I'll send you a quick summary after this call. And feel free to reach out anytime — I'm here."

## Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I don't have time to set this up right now" | "Totally understand — the setup actually takes less than an hour if we do it together right now. But if not, I can walk you through the 3 most important steps over email. Which do you prefer?" |
| "I'm not sure AI is reliable enough for my customers" | "That's a fair concern. The AI only answers questions it knows the answer to — when it's not sure, it transfers to a human. So there's always a safety net." |
| "What if AI gives wrong answers?" | "You're in control — you can test it before it goes live, and you can add corrections anytime. Most merchants spend about 30 min in the first week tweaking it, then it runs well." |
| "I only have a few visitors, is this worth it?" | "Even a few conversations a day — if AI converts one visitor who would've left without an answer, that often covers the plan cost. And it scales as you grow." |
| "I'm already on the free plan and don't want to upgrade" | "That's totally fine — let's get you set up on free first and see the results. You can always upgrade when you feel the limit." |
| "Can AI handle [specific complex scenario]?" | "For something that specific, the AI would likely transfer to a human rather than guess. That's by design — it keeps your brand protected." |
