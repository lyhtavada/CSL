# AM Call Talk Track — Joy Loyalty

> **Dùng cho:** Sonny, Alyssa
> **Mục đích:** Onboarding call + support call với ICP Joy merchants
> **Nguyên tắc:** Talk track, không phải script. Hiểu flow và intent — không đọc y chang.

---

## Before the Call (5 min prep)

Trước khi gọi, check nhanh merchant profile:

- [ ] Đang dùng plan gì? (Free / Professional / Advanced / Enterprise)
- [ ] Loyalty program đã launch chưa? (Published hay draft?)
- [ ] Earning rules đã setup chưa? (Place order rule đã on?)
- [ ] Có redemption rule chưa?
- [ ] Có customer nào đã earn points chưa?
- [ ] Ngày cài: bao lâu rồi mà chưa launch?
- [ ] Tên người liên hệ là ai?

**Mục tiêu call này là gì?**
- Onboarding: dẫn merchant launch được loyalty program, ra khỏi call với program live
- Support: unblock 1 vấn đề cụ thể, confirm merchant biết cách tự xử tiếp

---

## Call Flow

### 1. Opening (1–2 min)

> "Hi [Name], this is [your name] from the Joy Loyalty support team — how are you doing today?"

*(chờ họ trả lời)*

> "I'm reaching out because I noticed you [recently installed Joy / upgraded to [plan]] — I wanted to check in and make sure everything's set up the way you want it. Do you have about 15–20 minutes?"

**Nếu họ bận:**
> "No problem — is there a better time? I can also send you a setup guide by email if that's easier."

---

### 2. Discovery (3–5 min)

**Câu mở:**
> "Before we dive in — what made you decide to add a loyalty program? What are you hoping it'll do for your store?"

**Follow-up questions:**

| Tình huống | Câu hỏi |
|-----------|---------|
| Chưa biết tình trạng setup | "Have you had a chance to set up your earning and redeeming rules yet?" |
| Program draft, chưa launch | "What's been holding you back from publishing it?" |
| Đã launch nhưng không thấy kết quả | "Have any customers earned or redeemed points yet? How long has it been live?" |
| Migrate từ app khác | "What were you using before Joy? Was there a specific issue that made you switch?" |

**Red flags — note lại:**
- Kỳ vọng loyalty tăng revenue ngay lập tức → cần set expectation (loyalty is long game)
- Earning rule chưa match với business model → cần review
- Không có traffic / customer base → loyalty sẽ slow to show value

---

### 3. Setup Walkthrough (5–10 min)

**Dùng cho onboarding call. Skip nếu họ đã setup xong.**

**Mục tiêu:** Ra khỏi call với loyalty program live và ít nhất 1 earning + 1 redemption rule đang chạy.

#### Bước 1: Earning rule — Place Order
> "The most important rule to start with is the Place Order earning rule — this is how customers earn points every time they buy. Let's make sure that's on first."

- Đã có Place Order rule chưa? Đang on hay off?
- Reward type: points hay store credit?
- Confirm amount (VD: 1 point per $1 spent)

#### Bước 2: Redemption rule
> "Now let's set up how customers can spend their points. The most popular one is a discount — like 100 points = $5 off."

- Đã có redemption rule chưa?
- Discount amount hay percentage?
- Minimum points required?

#### Bước 3: Loyalty widget on storefront
> "Let's make sure the loyalty widget is showing on your store so customers can actually see their points."

- App embed on in Shopify theme editor?
- Widget showing on storefront?
- Loyalty landing page published?

#### Bước 4: Launch (nếu còn draft)
> "Looks like your program is set up but hasn't been published yet — do you want to go live right now? Once it's published, customers can start earning points on their next order."

---

### 4. Value Moment (2–3 min)

**Mục tiêu:** Connect setup với business outcome — đừng nói feature, nói result.

> "Once your loyalty program is live, every order your customers place earns them points — which brings them back to redeem. That cycle is what drives repeat purchases."

**Nếu họ mentioned pain point cụ thể:**

| Pain họ nói | Value frame |
|------------|------------|
| "Customers buy once and don't come back" | "That's exactly what loyalty solves — points give them a reason to come back instead of going to a competitor." |
| "Hard to compete on price" | "Loyalty shifts the value conversation away from price — customers stay because they've earned something with you." |
| "I want to reward my best customers" | "VIP tiers do exactly that — your top spenders get perks automatically, without you having to track it manually." |
| "I want to grow through referrals" | "Joy has a referral program built in — your existing customers bring new ones, and both get rewarded." |

---

### 5. Plan Features & Limits (1–2 min)

Nếu họ hỏi hoặc nếu setup bị block bởi plan limit:

| Feature | Plan cần |
|---------|---------|
| Migration từ app khác | Professional+ |
| VIP Tiers | Advanced+ |
| POS integration | Advanced+ |
| Multi-language (auto) | Advanced+ |
| Advanced referral + anti-cheat | Professional+ |

> "If [feature they want] is something you need, that's on the [plan] plan — it's [$X/mo]. Would you like me to walk you through what you'd get with that upgrade?"

---

### 6. Common Issues to Watch For

| Tình huống | Cần check |
|-----------|----------|
| Points không earn sau order | Earning rule on? Customer là member? Order đủ điều kiện (min spend, products)? |
| Discount không apply | Redemption rule on? Đủ points? Coupon chưa expire? Discount combination rules? |
| Widget không hiện | App embed on? Excluded on this page? |
| Referral không work | App Pixel enabled? Same email for claiming + ordering? Cookie 7 ngày chưa expire? |

---

### 7. Next Steps + Close (1–2 min)

**Onboarding call:**
> "So you're all set — your loyalty program is live and customers will start earning points from their next order. Over the next few weeks, keep an eye on the analytics to see who's earning and redeeming. If anything comes up, just reach out."

**Support call:**
> "We've sorted out [issue]. Is there anything else before I let you go?"

**Always:**
> "I'll send you a quick summary after this call. Feel free to message anytime — I'm here."

## Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I don't know how many points to give or what discount to offer" | "A good starting point is 1 point per $1 spent, and 100 points = $5 off. You can always adjust later based on how customers respond." |
| "Will this actually make customers come back?" | "Loyalty works when customers feel the reward is worth coming back for. We can tune the earning and redemption rates to make sure it feels valuable — not too easy, not out of reach." |
| "I'm worried customers will abuse the referral program" | "Joy has anti-cheat built in on Professional+ — it checks email, browser, and IP to catch fake referrals. That's one reason merchants upgrade." |
| "I want to migrate from [Smile / Yotpo / etc.]" | "Joy supports migration from those apps on Professional+ — we bring over customer name, email, points balance, VIP tier, and birthday. No one loses their points." |
| "I don't have time to manage this" | "Once the rules are set up, it runs automatically. You don't have to do anything day-to-day — customers earn and redeem on their own." |
| "What if a customer complains about their points?" | "You can do manual point adjustments from the Customer Management page — add or remove points with a note. It's quick." |
